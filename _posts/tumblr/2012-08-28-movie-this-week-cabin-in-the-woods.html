After reading great reviews of it, I had been eyeing the horror movie Cabin in the woods since long; and having finally watched it, i can say this: it’s Brilliant!!

                                             


For the record, i am not a huge follower of the horror genre, but i have seen enough films to make out the sometimes-subtle, often-inyourface satire in it.

There were so many occasions where you think you know the story, but the film is full of unexpected delights; horror and comical at the same time! 

The story is full of symbolism, waiting to be discovered, but even without it, the film doesn’t disappoint. It’s a must-must watch for horror fans. As for the others.., its a must watch!
