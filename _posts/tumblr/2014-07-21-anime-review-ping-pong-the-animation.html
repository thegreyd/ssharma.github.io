This is the first sports anime that i have watched, so essentially i have bypassed a lot of mediocre shows, and jumped straight on to a good one. 

I personally play ping pong so this show is special for me. The character dynamics of this show is superb; the imagery, symbolism is refreshing and exciting.

The background music fits perfectly in matches and in crucial moments. And the opening theme is catchy, something you can listen to again and again, one of my favorites.

The art style and animation are not typical anime, and are again refreshing from the all-too-similar-moe style. I was quite pleased with the matches, animated wonderfully. 

This is a story about Heroes and Monsters ; about Peco and Smile. A special mention to Kazama and Kong as being 2 great side characters of the show. Pretty much every character had a purpose and a back story.

Ping Pong the animation had a strong emotional impact on me. It is highly enjoyable. 

A sports fan or not, I would recommend it to everyone.
