Watching online videos, i often use Turn Off the Lights extension. While using it on Udacity (in Chrome), the video player frame shifts down awkwardly. Here’s a fix:

  • Install Stylish.
  • Go to -[Options]-[Write new Style].
  • Paste this line of css in [Code]  

#player-right-column{width:250px!important;} 

  • Click [Specify]. Choose [URLs on the domain]. Enter ‘udacity.com’ in adjacent text field.
  • Give it name 'udacity_lights_off’. Check [Enabled]. Hit [Save].

That’s it. Visually there is no difference. Now onto Coding.
