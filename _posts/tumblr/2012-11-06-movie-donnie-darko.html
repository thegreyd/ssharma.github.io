So, i hear you are a sci-fi movie fan. If so then this is a must watch movie for you. It has Time Travel.., and a troubled Teenager.., and cool Halloween costumes.., and school! What moar awesomeness do you need!? 

Donnie Darko, is undeniably a hard movie from the start. It is an intricate puzzle, by the director, to be understood and solved by the viewer. But it is not only sci-fi, its much more, having a plethora of emotions and hard hitting reality. The music is great and fitting; the acting is spot-on (Jake Gyllenhaal being amazing!).

I loved it, watching it the first time. And i missed quite a lot of things. I re-watched it ;and then truly loved it.

But, having said that, the reason anyone will hate it is for, it being, too hard to understand, too vague/subtle or being too bold.

At the end, it is a really rewarding and emotionally satisfying viewing experience. Go watch it!
