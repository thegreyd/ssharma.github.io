This happened to me 2 days ago.

Went to College. Sat at the last bench with a fellow boy. Environment-cum-Ecology period starts. This female teacher(25-30) starts babbling about Nuclear Energy. 

Asks questons about “what it is”. 

I raise my hand and speak something about nuclear fission, total mass of product less than the reactant, e=mc2. 

Apparantly, not satisfied enough. She says “Radioactivity” and asks questions like “why unstable nuclei?”, and “p/n ratio?”. She is (and had been) a Chemistry teacher. Noone answers. 

Tells students to come prepared next time. Says something about Radioactive decay. Next, starts to dictate. 

“Who discovered Radioactivity?”. 

“Marrie Curie and Peirre Curie”. 

Nope.“Enrico Fermi in 1936”.

REALLY? (Ofcourse not!)

“Fusion Energy is not currently possible, so we will only discuss Nuclear fission.”

Well..

“When a heavier radioactive nucleus breaks down into 2 smaller nucleus, the energy obtained is known as Nuclear Energy. […] Nuclear Energy is of 2 types - Fission and Fusion.”

(LOOLLL!!)snickers..

Again starts asking Questions.

I say to my partner, about the teacher being stupid and, her asking questions and not actually knowing the answers. Partner Smiles.

Notices it and the two of us, and goes on to scold us about being unattentive. Creates a big fuss.

No questions asked about the Nuclear Energy.

Says(threatens us) to prepare and to explain the relevant topics introduced, “full!”, in the next class(the next day).

Ok. Smug smile….

AFTERMATH:

Issues “Atomic and Nuclear Physics”, from the College Library.

Fires up wikipedia and starts reading. 

“Nucleus”, “RadioNuclei”, “Radioactive decay”, “Binding Energy”, “Mesons”, “Alpha decay”, “Gamma rays”, “Beta decay”, “Protons”, “Nuetrons”, “Nuclear fission”, “Nucleons”. “Nuetron Activation”, “Magic Numbers”, “Positron”, “Nuclear Energy”, “Half life period”, ….

The next day. Last bench.

Teacher enters class. Smiles and calls my name, inviting me to explain.

Heh.. Goes to the stage and starts explaining on the board. “Marie Curie and Peirre Curie discovered Radioactivity in 1898.”

“Why radioactivity? For that we need to delve into the nucleus and its stability”. Moar physics!

Some unsuccessful attempts of teacher to question. It is clearly not her domain.

I am in a hurry, don’t pay her any attention and continue to address the class.

She makes an attempt to call my partner, to come and explain. “Mam!”, but i smoothly say “ten minutes more, please.” All laugh. Her face turns red. 

I am on a roll. She didn’t expect this at all.

After, half hour of me being awesome, i rest my case.

She is pissed off. Like really! Scolds me for being “Disrespectful in addressing her”. 

Addresses class : “It was just a practice for being more confident, for future presentations.” “I gave you freedom, but you students abused it.” “From now on, i won’t provide any extra help, guidance, and everything will be strictly business.” Gives some Assignments. Leaves. 

Classmates flock towards me, shake my hand, congratulate me.

I won.
